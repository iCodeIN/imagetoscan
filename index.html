Frequently Asked Questions

What is ImageToScan?

ImageToScan is a service that allows you to convert images of documents into scanned copies in either an image format or PDF format. The tool also allows to correct brightness issues so that the scanned copy will be created in a crisp and clear manner.

How do you convert a photo of a document into a scanned copy?

Converting a photo of a document into a scanned copy is simple. Just follow the steps below:

  1. Drag and drop, or upload a photo of a document you wish to convert into a scan.
  2. Adjust the corners of the document.
  3. Adjust the brightness level of the scanned document.
  4. Download a scanned version of your document in either image or PDF format.
